        <h3 class="header"><strong>Teaching Assistantships</strong></h3>
<p>Students can receive funding through appointments as a Teaching Assistant (TA) or Reader. Information on responsibilities, requirements and benefits can be found on the Graduate Division website <a href="https://grad.uci.edu/funding/employment/teaching-assistantships.php" rel="noreferrer noopener" tabindex="-1" target="_blank" title="https://grad.uci.edu/funding/employment/teaching-assistantships.php">here</a>.</p>
<p>Interested ICS graduate students must submit an <a href="https://tad.ics.uci.edu/" rel="noreferrer noopener" tabindex="-1" target="_blank" title="https://tad.ics.uci.edu/">application</a><span> for each quarter they wish to search as a TA or Reader. The application requirement applies to ALL students, including those on a TA/Reader fellowship. </span></p>
<p>Students are notified of their appointments via email as soon as they are assigned. Any questions about TA and Reader assignments should be directed to the Department Managers.</p>
<br/>
<h3 class="header"><strong>Fellowships for ICS Graduate Students</strong></h3>
<p>Fellowship and award opportunities available to ICS Graduate Students are outlined below. This is a combined list of those offered by Graduate Division and external sources. Please see the links provided in the document for more information on each fellowship or award.</p>
<p><em>*Many fellowships have internal department deadlines 7-10 days prior to the official due date.</em></p>
