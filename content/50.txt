<div id="c-title">ICS Honors Program</div>
<blockquote>
<div id="c-text"><p>At the Bren School, highly ambitious, talented undergraduate students succeed in preparing themselves for complex and exciting careers. Education extends from the classroom to the laboratory, studio and library; undergraduate students have amazing opportunities to join faculty in research and have their work recognized in symposia and in print before graduating with a bachelor's degree. The ICS Honors Program, in particular, provides an opportunity for students enrolled in a Bren School major to make the most of their undergraduate career at UC Irvine.</p>
