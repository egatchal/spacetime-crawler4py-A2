<h1 style="white-space:pre-wrap;">Melissa Mazmanian, PhD</h1><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">I am a Chancellors Fellow at UCI,<a href="https://www.informatics.uci.edu/explore/faculty-profiles/melissa-mazmanian/"> Professor and Chair of the Department of Informatics</a>&nbsp;in the <a href="http://www.ics.uci.edu/">School of Information and Computer Sciences</a>,&nbsp;and <a href="https://merage.uci.edu/research-faculty/faculty-directory/Melissa-Mazmanian.html">Professor of Organization and Management</a>&nbsp;in the <a href="https://merage.uci.edu/">Paul Merage School of Management</a>&nbsp;(joint) at <a href="http://uci.edu/">University of California, Irvine.</a>&nbsp;</p><h2 style="white-space:pre-wrap;">AREAS OF FOCUS</h2><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">My interests revolve around the experience of communication technologies as used in-practice within organizational and personal contexts, specifically in relation to identity projection and the nature of time in the digital age. I have conducted a variety of ethnographic and qualitative <a href="http://www.ics.uci.edu/~mmazmani/Site/Research.html">research</a>&nbsp;projects on the individual experience and social dynamics that emerge when people adapt to using wireless modes of communication and have a series of <a href="http://www.ics.uci.edu/~mmazmani/Site/Publications.html">publications</a>&nbsp;on these topics.&nbsp;My work has also been of interest to the <a href="/press">popular press</a>.</p><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;"><a href="/bio"><strong>Click here for my CV and bio&nbsp;&gt;</strong></a></p><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">I teach a variety of <a href="http://www.ics.uci.edu/~mmazmani/Site/Courses.html">interdisciplinary courses</a>&nbsp;for the Department of Informatics and the Merage Business School and I have the pleasure of working with several excellent <a href="/students">doctoral students</a>.</p><h2 style="white-space:pre-wrap;">My Academic Background</h2><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">I earned my PhD in Organization Studies from the <a href="http://mitsloan.mit.edu/">MIT Sloan School of Management</a>&nbsp;in 2009 where I worked with <a href="https://mitsloan.mit.edu/faculty/detail.php?in_spseqno=41293">Wanda Orlikowski</a>&nbsp;and <a href="https://mitsloan.mit.edu/faculty/detail.php?in_spseqno=41395">JoAnne Yates</a>. I received my MSI in Information Economics, Management and Policy from the <a href="http://www.si.umich.edu/">University of Michigan School of Information</a>&nbsp;in 2002. I also completed a BA in 1997 in English Literature and Creative Writing at <a href="https://www.coloradocollege.edu/">Colorado College</a>.</p><h2 style="white-space:pre-wrap;">POsitions I hold</h2><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">In the Department of Informatics I currently serve as the <a href="https://www.informatics.uci.edu/grad/overview/">Department Chair</a>. At UCI I also serve as the Co-Director for the <a href="http://www.cor.web.uci.edu/">Center for Organizational Research (COR)</a><a href="http://www.socsci.uci.edu/~ethnog/">.</a></p><h2
