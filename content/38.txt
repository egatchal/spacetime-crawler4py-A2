<div id="c-text"><h2>Fall 2022: ICS Course Restrictions</h2>
<p>&#65279;This timeline for ICS course restrictions updates is being provided to help students with planning during the open enrollment period.</p>
<p><strong>It is highly recommended that students check this page periodically. </strong> The timeline will be followed for the most part, but course restrictions are subject to change<em>,</em> such as in the case of highly impacted courses where restrictions may be extended or adjusted at a time other than initially planned.</p>
<p>ICS Student Affairs reserves the right to adjust restrictions and the timeline at any time.</p>
<hr style="width: 100%;" width="100%"/>
<p><strong>Important Notes:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>ICS Graduate Students who wish to enroll in a restricted ICS course (e.g., a Stats M.S. or Ph.D. student wishing to take CS 284A) may contact an ICS graduate counselor for assistance.</li>
<li>ICS&#160;<em>strictly</em>&#160;enforces the campus' Week 2 add/drop deadline. The add/drop deadline for Fall 2022 is&#160;<strong>5:00pm, Friday, October 7, 2022</strong>.</li>
<li>Graduate students outside of the School of ICS must wait enroll in ICS courses beginning <strong>12:00pm, September 1</strong><strong>, 2022</strong>. No exceptions to this registration schedule will be made.</li>
<li>ICS Graduate Students wishing to enroll in an ICS&#160;<span><span style="text-decoration: underline;">undergraduate</span> level</span>&#160;course may send an email requesting authorization to&#160;<em>ucounsel@uci.edu</em>&#160;beginning <strong>12:00pm, September 1</strong><strong>, 2022</strong>.</li>
<li>ICS&#160;<span>Undergraduate Students</span>&#160;seeking to take an ICS graduate level course and receives permission from the instructor may email&#160;<em>gcounsel@ics.uci.edu</em>&#160;for assistance beginning <strong>12:00pm, September 1</strong><strong>, 2022</strong>.</li>
</ul>
<hr style="width: 100%;" width="100%"/>
<p class="text"></p>
<p class="text"><strong><span style="color: #ff8000;"><a id="GradRestrictions"></a>&#65279;School/Major restrictions on <span style="text-decoration: underline;">GRADUATE</span> courses</span> will follow the below timeline:</strong></p>

<p><strong>5/16/22</strong><br/>Spring 2022<br/>Week 8</p>
<p><span style="color: #007acc;"><strong>School/Major restrictions active</strong></span></p>
</td>
<td>
<p><em><strong>COMPSCI: </strong></em>Restricted to CS, NS, SE, and STATS graduate students only, <strong><em>except</em></strong>:</p>
<ul>
<li style="margin-left: -10px;">CS 242, CS 261: CS, CmptSci, SE, NS graduate students only</li>
<li style="margin-left: -10px;">CS 284A: CS, CmptSci, SE, MCSB graduate students only</li>
<li style="margin-left: -10px;">All courses with suffix 'P', e.g., CS 201P: restricted to students in the MCS and/or MDS programs <em>only</em></li>
</ul>
<p><em><strong>DATA</strong></em>: Restricted to students in the MDS program only.</p>
<p><em><strong>IN4MATX</strong></em>: Restricted to INF, SE, CS, and STATS graduate students only, <strong><em>except</em></strong>:</p>
<ul style="margin-left: -10px;">
<li>INF 280, INF 281, INF 282: Restricted to students in the MHCID program <em>only</em></li>
</ul>
<p><em><strong>NET SYS: </strong></em>Restricted to NS graduate students only</p>
<p><em><strong>STATS: </strong></em>Restricted to Stats graduate students only,<strong> </strong><em><strong>except:</strong></em></p>
<ul>
<li style="margin-left: -10px;">Stats 201, Stats 281A: All graduate students <span style="text-decoration: underline;">except</span> Statistics</li>
<li style="margin-left: -10px;">All coures with suffix 'P', e.g., Stat 200AP: restricted to students in the MDS program only</li>
</ul>
<p><em><strong>SWE: </strong></em>Restricted to INF, SE, CS, and STATS Stats graduate students only,<strong> </strong><em><strong>except:</strong></em></p>
<ul>
<li style="margin-left: -10px;">All courses with suffix 'P', e.g., SWE 241P: restricted to students in the MSWE program only</li>
</ul>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>
<p><strong>9/1/2022</strong></p>
<p><span style="color: #007acc;"><strong>School/Major restrictions lifted</strong></span></p>
</td>

<ul>

<li>Graduate students may begin requesting permission to enroll in open, ICS undergraduate level courses. Send inquiries to <em><a href="mailto:ucounsel@uci.edu">ucounsel@uci.edu</a></em></li>
