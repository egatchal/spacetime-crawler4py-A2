<section class="scroll-margin-top" id="Initial_Plan">
<p dir="ltr"><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>&#65279;Initial Plan for the Honors Project</strong></span></p>
<p dir="ltr"><span>Students need to submit an Initial Plan once they have found a Faculty Advisor.&#160;</span></p>
<p dir="ltr">Here are the steps to submit your Initial Plan:&#160;</p>
<ul>
<li aria-level="1" dir="ltr">
<p dir="ltr" role="presentation"><span>Step 1: Download the </span><a href="https://www.ics.uci.edu/ugrad/honors/pdf/forms/Initial-Plan-for-the-Honors-Project.pdf"><span>Initial Plan form</span></a><span>&#160;and fill out the student portion.&#160;</span></p>
</li>
<li aria-level="1" dir="ltr">
<p dir="ltr" role="presentation"><span>Step 2: Have your Faculty Advisor electronically sign the </span><a href="https://www.ics.uci.edu/ugrad/honors/pdf/forms/Initial-Plan-for-the-Honors-Project.pdf"><span>Initial Plan form</span></a></p>
</li>
<li aria-level="1" dir="ltr">
<p dir="ltr" role="presentation"><span>Step 3: Once the form is completed, please submit it through the </span><a href="https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScCeVmQU8_v72A-fY8wG3kbD7G4VKFDc_uVjDNDUhy-6zqA7A/viewform?usp=sf_link"><span>Initial Plan Submission Google Form</span></a><span>.</span></p>
</li>
</ul>
</section>
<section class="scroll-margin-top" id="Faculty_Contact">
<p><span><span>&#160;</span></span></p>
<p dir="ltr"><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>&#65279;Faculty Contact Form for the Honors Project</strong></span></p>
<p dir="ltr"><span>If you do not have a Faculty Advisor by the end of Fall quarter, that is okay. We will give you extra time to find one. However, we will require you to make at least 3 faculty contacts each quarter until you do find a Faculty Advisor. You can find more information on our </span><a href="overview/step-3-finding-a-faculty-advisor-and-taking-cs-h198.php"><span>website</span></a><span>.&#160;</span></p>
<p><span><span>&#160;</span></span>Here are the steps to submit your Faculty Contact Form:</p>
<ul>
<li aria-level="1" dir="ltr">
<p dir="ltr" role="presentation"><span>Step 1: Download the </span><a href="https://www.ics.uci.edu/ugrad/honors/pdf/forms/Faculty-contact-report-form.pdf"><span>Faculty Contact Form</span></a><span>.</span></p>
</li>
<li aria-level="1" dir="ltr">
<p dir="ltr" role="presentation"><span>Step 2: Fill and sign the </span><a href="https://www.ics.uci.edu/ugrad/honors/pdf/forms/Faculty-contact-report-form.pdf"><span>Faculty Contact Form</span></a><span>.</span></p>
</li>
<li aria-level="1" dir="ltr">
<p dir="ltr" role="presentation">Step 3: Once the form is completed, please submit it via email to the <a href="contact-us.php">ICS Honors Program Academic Counselor</a>.&#160;</p>
</li>
</ul>
</section>
<section class="scroll-margin-top" id="Final_Project">
<p><span><span>&#160;</span></span></p>
<p dir="ltr"><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>&#65279;Final Project Form &amp; Thesis</strong></span></p>
<p dir="ltr"><span>Once you have completed your thesis, you will need to submit it to the ICS Undergraduate Student Affairs Office along with the Final Project Form.&#160;</span></p>
<p><span><span>&#160;</span></span>Here are the steps to submit your Final Project Form:</p>
<ul>
<li aria-level="1" dir="ltr">
<p dir="ltr" role="presentation"><span>Step 1: Download the </span><a href="Remote Final Thesis Form.pdf"><span>Final Project Form</span></a><span> on our website and fill out the student portion.&#160;</span></p>
</li>
<li aria-level="1" dir="ltr">
<p dir="ltr" role="presentation"><span>Step 2: Have your Faculty Advisor electronically sign the </span><a href="Remote Final Thesis Form.pdf"><span>Final Project Form</span></a><span>.</span></p>
<ul>
<li aria-level="2" dir="ltr">
<p dir="ltr" role="presentation"><span>Note: You don't need to worry about the Honors Program Advisor signature. We will reach out to them on your behalf.</span></p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li aria-level="1" dir="ltr">
<p dir="ltr" role="presentation"><span>Step 3: Once the form is completed, please submit it through the </span><a href="https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSft8Rmm9dWiENP3ON6BIWhegNLlFCr9ol4hGwPYxI0yDG1QSw/viewform?usp=sf_link"><span>Final Project Form Submission Google Form</span></a><span> along with your thesis.</span></p>
</li>
</ul>
<p dir="ltr"><span>Once you have submitted your thesis, we will forward it to the ICS Honors Program Advisor for review. We will contact you once we have received an update. Please note that this process may take a few weeks.</span></p>
</section>
