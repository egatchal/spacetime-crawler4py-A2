<div id="c-text"><h4 class="header">Opportunities for ICS Students</h4>
<p>As members of the UCI community, all ICS students have access to a wide range of campus resources (a partial list of which can be viewed <a href="Campus_Resources.php" target="_blank" title="General UCI Resources">here</a>). In addition to these, there are also opportunities for engagement and enrichment geared specifically towards ICS students.</p>
<p>ICS students are encouraged to complement their academics with extra-curricular activities such as research or joining a student organization.&#160; Benefits of this include, but are not limited to: networking with other students, faculty and companies; participating in social activities; forming study groups and friendships; attending various workshops and presentations; gaining deeper experience and knowledge of your chosen field; and more.</p>

<p>&#160;</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #6699cc;"><strong>&#187;</strong></span> <a id="themehouse"></a>&#65279;ICS Theme House in Arroyo Vista<br/></strong></p>
<p>The Arroyo Vista theme house provides informal, academic and social functions for students interested in the field of information and computer science. The goals of this house are to expose residents to faculty, alumni and businesspersons in the information and computer sciences field.</p>
<p>Activities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>visits to corporations</li>
<li>opportunities to participate in symposiums and workshops centering on academic success</li>
<li>community and career opportunities</li>
<li>preparation for graduate/professional schools and learning skills</li>
<li>social activities</li>
</ul>
<p>The house is open to all students with an interest in information and computer sciences.</p>
<p>&#160;</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #6699cc;"><strong>&#187;<a id="honors"></a>&#65279;</strong></span> Honors Opportunities</strong></p>
<p>Students may apply to be in the ICS Honors Program during Spring quarter if they meet certain academic criteria and have the support of faculty.&#160; Benefits of being in the ICS Honors Program include networking with other ICS Honors students, conducting research with a faculty mentor, and working on a thesis<strong>.<br/></strong></p>
<ul>
<li><a href="../honors/index.php" target="_blank" title="ICS Honors Program">Bren School of ICS Honors Program</a></li>
<li><a href="http://www.honors.uci.edu/" target="_blank" title="CHP">Campuswide Honors Program</a></li>
</ul>
<p>&#160;</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #6699cc;"><strong>&#187;</strong></span> <a id="research"></a>&#65279;Research Opportunities<br/></strong></p>
<p>All Bren ICS majors are encouraged to take advantage of this valuable experience. Faculty advertise many research opportunities every year.</p>
<ul>
<li>Independent Study: Enroll in an independent study (199 course number) under a faculty advisor unit credit</li>
<li><a href="http://www.urop.uci.edu/" target="_blank" title="UROP">UROP</a>: The Undergraduate Research Opportunities Program encourages and facilitates research and creative activities by undergraduates from all schools and academic disciplines at UCI. Programs include <a href="http://www.urop.uci.edu/SURP.html" target="_blank" title="SURP">SURP</a>, <a href="http://www.urop.uci.edu/surf-it.html" target="_blank" title="SURF-IT">SURF-IT</a>, <a href="http://www.urop.uci.edu/mdp.html" target="_blank" title="MDP">MDP</a>, and others.</li>
</ul>
<p>&#160;</p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #6699cc;"><strong>&#187;</strong></span> <a id="scholarships"></a>&#65279;ICS Scholarships </strong></p>

<li>To search for more scholarships, visit the <a href="http://www.ofas.uci.edu/content/Scholarships.aspx" target="_blank" title="Financial Aid: Scholarships">Financial Aid and Scholarships website</a>.</li>
</ul>
