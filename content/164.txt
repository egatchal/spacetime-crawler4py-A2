<li><b>The graphs of planar soap bubbles</b>.<br>
D. Eppstein.<br>
<a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/1207.3761">arXiv:1207.3761</a>.<br>
<a href="https://doi.org/10.1145/2462356.2462370"><i>Proc. 29th ACM Symp. on Computational Geometry</i>, Rio de Janeiro,
2013, pp. 27&ndash;36</a>.
<p>
We characterize the graphs of two-dimensional soap bubble clusters as
being exactly the bridgeless 3-regular planar graphs.
The proof uses the M&ouml;bius invariance of the properties
characterizing these clusters together with our previous circle packing
method for constructing Lombardi drawings of graphs.
<p>
For the journal version, see "<a href="p-lombardi-soap.html">A
M&ouml;bius-invariant power diagram and its applications to soap
bubbles and planar lombardi drawing.</a>".
